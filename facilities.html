About Mahabaleshwar & Our Resort

Situated 1,372 metres above sea level and just 275 km from Mumbai, Mahabaleshwar is a paradise for nature lovers. Cool climate, stunning viewpoints, and evergreen forests make it India’s most loved hill station.

At Hotel Saket Plaza, we combine Nature + Luxury to give you a memorable holiday experience. Perfect for families, couples, and corporate groups seeking comfort with world-class hospitality.

Nature existed. We added luxury.
