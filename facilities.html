A Sanctuary of Comfort

Situated 1,372 metres above sea level, Mahabaleshwar is a paradise for those seeking a breath of fresh mountain air. At Hotel Saket Plaza, we have curated a selection of facilities that transform a simple stay into a luxury retreat.

Whether you are visiting for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, our world-class hospitality ensures that every moment is spent in total relaxation and sophisticated comfort.

"Nature provided the canvas; we added the luxury."
