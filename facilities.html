Nature + Luxury

Situated 1,372 metres above sea level and just 275 km from Mumbai, Mahabaleshwar is a paradise for nature lovers. Cool climate, stunning viewpoints, evergreen forests, and peaceful surroundings make it one of India’s most loved hill stations.

At Hotel Saket Plaza, we combine the raw beauty of the mountains with sophisticated comfort to give you a memorable holiday experience. Perfect for families, couples, corporate groups and travellers seeking world-class hospitality.

"Nature existed. We added luxury."
