Hotel Saket Plaza

Mahabaleshwar's Colorful Luxury Retreat

✨ Where Nature Meets Luxury

Situated 1,372 metres above sea level and just 275 km from Mumbai, Mahabaleshwar is a paradise for nature lovers. Cool climate, stunning viewpoints, evergreen forests, and peaceful surroundings make it one of India’s most loved hill stations.

At Hotel Saket Plaza, we combine Nature + Luxury to give you a memorable holiday experience. Our multi-coloured swimming pool, waterfall features, and beautifully landscaped gardens add a splash of color to your stay. Nature existed; we simply added luxury.
