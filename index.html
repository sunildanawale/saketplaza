✨ Where Nature Meets Luxury

Nestled in the lush green hills of the Sahyadri range in Maharashtra lies the beautiful hill station of Mahabaleshwar.

Hotel Saket Plaza is one of the finest attractions—one more reason to visit this enchanting destination. Surrounded by greenery and within walking distance from the market, the hotel offers a serene yet convenient stay. Whether you are honeymooners, families, groups, or conference delegates, Hotel Saket Plaza ensures a delightful experience for every guest.

A multi-coloured swimming pool, waterfall features, a machan (tree house), colourful fountains, and beautifully landscaped gardens—our list of attractions is truly captivating. We offer a variety of tastefully designed rooms with facilities that exceed typical 3-star expectations.

Visit us and experience it yourself. Nature existed; we added luxury.
