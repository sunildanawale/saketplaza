✨ Where Nature Meets Luxury — Hotel Saket Plaza ✨

Nestled in the lush green hills of the Sahyadri range in Maharashtra lies the beautiful hill station of Mahabaleshwar.

Hotel Saket Plaza is one of the finest attractions—one more reason to visit this enchanting destination. Surrounded by greenery and within walking distance from the market, the hotel offers a serene yet convenient stay.

A multi-coloured swimming pool, waterfall features, and beautifully landscaped gardens—our list of attractions is truly captivating. We offer tastefully designed rooms that exceed expectations.

Visit us and experience it yourself. Nature existed; we added luxury.
