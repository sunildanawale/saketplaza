Where Nature Meets Luxury

Nestled in the lush green hills of the Sahyadri range, Hotel Saket Plaza stands as a testament to Mahabaleshwar’s enchanting beauty. We provide a sanctuary where the air is purer, and the greenery is endless.

Located within walking distance of the local market yet tucked away in serene surroundings, our retreat offers the perfect balance of convenience and tranquility.

From our unique multi-coloured swimming pool and cascading waterfalls to our meticulously landscaped gardens, every corner of Saket Plaza is designed to exceed your expectations.

"Nature existed; we simply added the luxury."
